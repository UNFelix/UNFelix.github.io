Our Solution
BrainVu uses a non-invasive and remote mobile/AR/VR camera to extract physiological bio-markers that indicate changes in brain activities and extract human emotional responses including: Mental Load, Stress Level and Emotional Engagement. Using these responses BrainVu can connect to a multitude of platforms and provide emotional and mental representation for those responses.

Technology
Capture of Psychological Bio Markers
BrainVu analyzes a spectrum of unique eye measurements to identify changes in alertness, attention i.e. mental effort. To understand the consumer’s tendency toward a certain subject by reenacting sensory and emotional experiences (such as taste, video and more) BrainVu uses a set of questions and responses measured by the system.
Model-Based Algorythm for Mental Analysis
By correlating the participants emotional response to a specific event in time or the subject’s gaze BrainVu can identify which element creates the impact.
Real Time Emotional Representation
By combining the indices developed together with additional bio markers such as HRV, Gaze, facial coding, response time and others BrainVu has created the BrainVu Sentiment Index. It gauges both tendency towards a specific products/touch points as well as the emotional engagement (stress excitement, mental load) of the consumer during the research experience.
